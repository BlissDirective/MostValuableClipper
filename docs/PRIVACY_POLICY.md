## 6. Data Storage and Security

### 6.1 Storage Locations
- **Primary database:** Supabase (US-based, with EU options available)
- **Video storage:** Cloudflare R2 (global edge network)
- **Processing compute:** Modal (US-based cloud infrastructure)
- **Queue data:** Upstash Redis (global)

### 6.2 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption in transit:** All data transmitted via TLS 1.3
- **Encryption at rest:** Database encryption, encrypted storage buckets
- **Secure authentication:** JWT tokens, OAuth 2.0, password hashing (bcrypt)
- **Access controls:** Role-based access, least-privilege principles
- **Regular audits:** Security reviews, dependency updates
- **Token security:** Social media OAuth tokens encrypted at rest

### 6.3 Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period | Deletion |
|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| Account information | Until account deletion | 30 days after deletion request |
| Source videos | 90 days (configurable) | Automatic or user-initiated |
| Generated clips | Until account deletion | User can delete anytime |
| Transcripts | Indefinite (for reference) | Deleted with account |
| Social tokens | Until disconnect or expiry | Immediate upon disconnect |
| Payment records | 7 years (legal requirement) | Anonymized after 7 years |
| Usage logs | 90 days | Automatic |
| AI training data | Only if cohort opt-in enabled | Removed upon opt-out |

**Source Video Retention:** You can configure retention periods per pipeline (30, 90, or 365 days). Default is 90 days. Transcripts and generated clips are retained indefinitely unless you delete them.

### 6.4 Data Breach Procedures
In the event of a data breach:
- We will notify affected users within **72 hours** of discovery
- We will report to relevant supervisory authorities as required by law
- We will take immediate steps to contain and remediate the breach
- We will provide guidance on protective measures users should take

### 8.1 AI-Generated Content
Our Service uses artificial intelligence to:
- Analyze video content and identify clip-worthy moments
- Generate captions, titles, and descriptions
- Create transcripts from audio
- Suggest optimal posting strategies

### 8.2 Content Ownership
- **Your original content:** You retain all rights to source videos you upload or link
- **AI-generated clips:** The clips generated by our Service are considered derivative works. You are granted a license to use, distribute, and monetize these clips
- **Platform requirements:** Some social media platforms require disclosure of AI-generated content. Our Service can automatically add disclosure labels where required

### 8.3 Cohort Learning (Opt-In Only)
Our Service offers an optional "cohort learning" feature:
- **What it does:** Uses anonymized, aggregated patterns from similar users to improve clip recommendations for new users
- **Default status:** **OFF by default** — you must explicitly opt in
- **What is shared:** Only anonymized statistical patterns, never your raw videos or personal data
- **How to opt out:** Settings → Privacy → Cohort Learning → Toggle OFF
- **Effect of opt-out:** No impact on Service functionality; only affects the quality of initial recommendations

### 8.4 AI Transparency
- We clearly indicate when content has been AI-generated or AI-assisted
- We provide information about the AI models used (Claude, Whisper, etc.)
- We do not use AI to make decisions that significantly affect users without human oversight
